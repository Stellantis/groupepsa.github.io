### `Device.GetUIN()`

| **Description** | Get the UIN
| **Response** | *String* UIN
| **Parameter**   | *Void*

#### Example

```javascript
var UIN = Device.GetUIN()
// UIN = "0A12345B67C8D9E012FG"
```
