Welcome to the WebPortal section. Webportal is the browser embedded in Stellantis connected vehicles (Citroën, DS, Peugeot, Opel and Vauxhall). It allows to build embbeded Applications in the vehicle infotainment system. This api exists under two different versions, webportal v1 also known as NAC have been implemented in vehicles from february 2017 ; Webportal v2 also call IVI is implemanted in vehicles starting middle 2021. In both cases, you will be able to retrieve data, interact with the vehicle infotainment system, navigation & send message to the vehicle from our back-end. Choose your version and start your tour by reading the doc!
